DENKI BRAN은 ⚡️와 🥃에서 이름을 따온 일본의 칵테일입니다.
여전히 교토 어딘가에서 전승되고 있으며, 인기가 한창일때는 가짜전기브란이 유통되기도 했습니다.
한 거룩한 게으름뱅이의 이야기에 따르면 원래 이름이 덴구브란이라고 하지만, 정확한 것은 아무도 모릅니다.

DENKI BRAN is a Japanese cocktail that gets its name from the words “electric” and “cocktail,” and is sometimes referred to as “electronic brandy.” Although its popularity has waned, it is still available in some bars in Kyoto in the 21st century. At one point, it was so popular that a similar cocktail called “Fake Denki Bran” was created.
